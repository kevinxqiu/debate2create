## Mission
Design a robot morphology optimized for the task: {task_description}

## Environment
The swimmer is a planar robot consisting of three connected segments (links) forming a linear chain. Two rotary joints connect the segments. The robot is suspended in a viscous fluid and propels itself forward by applying torque to the joints, creating undulatory motion.

## Parameters
Output 6 positive parameters that define the robot structure:

**Segment Lengths:**
- `param1`: Length of first segment (front/leading segment)
- `param2`: Length of second segment (middle segment)
- `param3`: Length of third segment (rear/trailing segment)

**Capsule Radii** (size of each segment):
- `param4`: First segment capsule radius
- `param5`: Second segment capsule radius
- `param6`: Third segment capsule radius
